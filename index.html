About TechPearl MW

Building a thriving community of women in technology

Our Mission

TechPearl MW is dedicated to bridging the gender gap in technology by providing young women and girls with access to quality education, mentorship, and opportunities in the tech industry.

We believe that diversity in technology drives innovation and creates better solutions for everyone. Through our programs, we're building a supportive community where women can thrive and lead in tech.
